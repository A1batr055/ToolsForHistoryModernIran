Ahmad Shah 艾哈迈德·沙阿 1896–1929
Last Qajar monarch · 卡扎尔王朝末代君主
卡扎尔
Ascended the throne in 1909 as a minor; did not come of age until 1914. Lacking real power and fearful for his life, he left Iran soon after the 1921 coup. Died in Paris; buried in Karbala.
1909年以幼主身份继位，1914年方才成年。因缺乏实权且担忧自身安全，于1921年政变后不久离开伊朗，后卒于巴黎，葬于卡尔巴拉。
Ahmadinejad, Mahmud 马哈茂德·艾哈迈迪内贾德 1956–
Conservative president (2005) · 保守派总统（2005年当选）
共和国
Son of a blacksmith and veteran of the Iran–Iraq War. Won the presidency on populist themes, promising to distribute oil wealth, revive Khomeini's revolutionary ideals, and deliver a final blow to the "one thousand families" who supposedly ruled Iran. Supported by the most conservative ulama.
铁匠之子，两伊战争老兵。以民粹主义纲领赢得大选，承诺将石油财富分配给人民、复兴霍梅尼革命理想、终结据称统治伊朗数百年的"一千个家族"的权势。获最保守派乌莱玛支持。
Alam, Asadallah 阿萨多拉·阿拉姆 1919–1978
Main confidant of Muhammad Reza Shah · 穆罕默德·礼萨·沙阿首席密友
巴列维
From a long line of notables in Sistan and Baluchestan known as the "Lords of the Marches." Joined the court in 1946 and advised the shah until his death at the beginning of the revolution. Some speculate his absence explains the shah's vacillations in 1977–78. His posthumously published memoirs, however, support the view that he was part of the larger problem.
出身锡斯坦-俾路支斯坦世代权贵家族，人称"边陲之主"。1946年入宫，此后一直担任国王顾问直至革命前夕去世。有人推测，正是阿拉姆的缺席导致了国王在1977–78年间的优柔寡断，继而促成革命。然而，其身后出版的回忆录表明，他本人也是更大问题的一部分。
Alavi, Bozorg 博佐尔格·阿拉维 1904–1995
Leading figure in modern Persian literature · 现代波斯文学代表人物
知识分子 巴列维
Educated in Germany; co-edited Donya; imprisoned for belonging to Arani's circle. On release in 1941, helped found the Tudeh Party. Among his works: the prison memoirs The Fifty-Three. Influenced by Kafka, Freud, Hemingway, and Marx. A close friend of Sadeq Hedayat.
留学德国，曾参与编辑《世界》（Donya）杂志，因参加阿拉尼学术圈入狱。1941年获释后参与创建人民党（图德党）。代表作包括狱中回忆录《五十三人》。受卡夫卡、弗洛伊德、海明威及马克思影响。与文学巨匠萨德格·赫达亚特交好。
Al-e Ahmad, Jalal 贾拉勒·阿勒·艾哈迈德 1923–1969
Initiator of the "back to roots" movement · "回归根源"运动的发起人
知识分子 革命
Began as a Marxist in the Tudeh Party; remained an intellectual skeptic, but in the 1960s increasingly sought Iran's cultural roots in Shi'ism. Best-known work: Gharbzadegi (lit. "Struck by the West") — arguing Iran was being destroyed by a "plague coming from the West." One of the few intellectuals openly praised by Khomeini.
早年以马克思主义者身份加入人民党，终身保持知识分子的怀疑立场，但1960年代日益转向在什叶派中寻找伊朗文化根基。代表作《西方迷醉》（Gharbzadegi，字面义"被西方所击"），主张伊朗正被"来自西方的瘟疫"所摧毁。系霍梅尼公开赞扬的少数知识分子之一。
Arani, Taqi 塔基·阿拉尼 1902–1940
Father of Marxism in Iran · 伊朗马克思主义之父
知识分子 巴列维
Educated in Germany (1922–30); returned to launch the journal Donya and form an intellectual circle whose members later founded the Tudeh Party. Sentenced to ten years for advocating "socialism" and "atheism"; died in prison.
1922–30年留学德国，归国后创办《世界》杂志并组建知识分子团体，其成员后来创建了人民党。因鼓吹"社会主义"和"无神论"被判处十年监禁，死于狱中。
Ashraf, Princess 阿什拉芙公主 1919–2016
The shah's twin sister · 国王的孪生姐妹
巴列维
A forceful personality who played an important role behind the scenes, helping many young Western-educated technocrats attain wealth and high office. Some feel she epitomized the worst features of the regime; others claim this is a misogynist's view.
性格强势，在幕后发挥重要作用，帮助许多受西方教育的年轻技术官僚获取财富和高位。有人认为她是旧政权最恶劣特征的化身，也有人认为这种评价带有厌女偏见。
Bahar, Muhammad Taqi (Malek al-Shu'ara) 穆罕默德·塔基·巴哈尔（"诗人之王"） 1885–1952
Poet laureate of classical Persian literature · 波斯古典文学桂冠诗人
知识分子 卡扎尔
Began political life as an active member of the constitutional movement; died as president of the pro-Tudeh Peace Partisans. Wrote the well-known Short History of Political Parties in Iran.
以立宪运动积极成员的身份步入政坛，最终以亲人民党的"和平拥护者"组织主席身份辞世。著有名作《伊朗政党简史》。
Bani-Sadr, Sayyed Abul-Hassan 赛义德·阿布·哈桑·巴尼萨德尔 1933–
Iran's first president · 伊朗伊斯兰共和国首任总统
革命 共和国
Son of an ayatollah who had supported the 1953 coup, but Bani-Sadr sided with Mossadeq and was active in Paris in the National Front and Liberation Movement. Returned with Khomeini in 1979; briefly served as president before accusing the clergy of scheming to establish a "dictatorship of the mullahtariat." Had to escape back to Paris.
其父为支持1953年政变的阿亚图拉，但本人站在摩萨台一方，长期流亡巴黎，活跃于民族阵线和解放运动。1979年随霍梅尼回国，短暂出任总统，后指控教士阶层图谋建立"毛拉专政"（mullahtariat），被迫再次流亡巴黎。
Bazargan, Mehdi 迈赫迪·巴扎尔甘 1907–1995
Khomeini's first prime minister · 霍梅尼任命的首位总理
革命 共和国
Deputy minister under Mossadeq; far more religious than his National Front colleagues. Founded the Liberation Movement in 1961, committed to Iranian nationalism, Western liberalism, and Shi'i Islam. Secularists deemed him too religious; the religious deemed him too secularist. Resigned to protest the 1979 embassy seizure.
曾在摩萨台政府任副部长，比民族阵线同僚虔诚得多。1961年创建解放运动，致力于融合伊朗民族主义、西方自由主义与什叶派伊斯兰。世俗派嫌其太宗教，宗教派嫌其太世俗。1979年为抗议学生占领美国使馆而辞去总理职务。
Behbehani, Sayyed Abdallah 赛义德·阿卜杜拉·贝赫贝哈尼 1844–1910
Ayatollah prominent in the Constitutional Revolution · 立宪革命中的重要阿亚图拉
教士 卡扎尔
One of two ayatollahs prominent in the Constitutional Revolution. Assassinated in the fights between secular Democrats and religious Moderates. His son, Ayatollah Muhammad Behbehani, actively supported the 1953 coup — the bazaar money for the coup was known as "Behbehani dollars."
立宪革命中两位核心阿亚图拉之一。在世俗民主派与宗教温和派的冲突中遭暗杀。其子穆罕默德·贝赫贝哈尼阿亚图拉积极支持1953年政变——政变中在巴扎撒出的收买资金被称为"贝赫贝哈尼美元"。
Boroujerdi, Ayatollah Aqa Hajj Aqa Hussein Tabatabai 阿亚图拉·博鲁杰尔迪 1875–1961
Last paramount Shi'i leader · 最后一位至高无上的什叶派领袖
教士 巴列维
Long seminary career in Najaf and Boroujerd; moved to Qom in 1944 where he became the supreme marja-e taqled. Although he frowned on clerics in politics, he turned a blind eye to those who helped the 1953 coup. His death prompted younger grand ayatollahs to compete for his position — and the shah to launch the White Revolution.
长期在纳杰夫和博鲁杰尔德的神学院任教，1944年迁至库姆，成为最高"效仿之源"（marja-e taqled）。虽反对教士参政，却对支持1953年政变的教士睁一只眼闭一只眼。其去世引发年轻大阿亚图拉争夺其地位，也促使国王发动白色革命。
Curzon, Lord George 乔治·寇松勋爵 1859–1925
British foreign minister obsessed with Iran · 痴迷于伊朗的英国外交大臣
外国 卡扎尔
So enamored of Iran that he tried to incorporate it into his empire. Published the classic Persia and the Persian Question. His Anglo-Persian Agreement of 1919 created a nationalist backlash in Iran.
对伊朗着迷到试图将其纳入大英帝国版图。著有经典之作《波斯与波斯问题》。其主导的1919年英波协定在伊朗引发了民族主义的强烈反弹。
Dehkhoda, Ali Akbar 阿里·阿克巴尔·德赫胡达 1879–1956
Leading intellectual of the Constitutional Revolution · 立宪革命核心知识分子
知识分子 卡扎尔
A biting satirist during the Constitutional Revolution who aroused much opposition from clergy and landed class. Withdrew from politics to compile his famous Loqatnameh (Lexicon). In August 1953, when the shah fled, some radical nationalists offered him the presidency of their prospective republic.
立宪革命时期辛辣的讽刺作家，引起教士阶层和地主阶级的强烈反对。后退出政坛，毕生编纂其著名的《德赫胡达辞典》（Loqatnameh）。1953年8月国王出逃时，部分激进民族主义者曾拟推举他出任共和国总统。
Ebadi, Shiren 希琳·艾巴迪 1947–
Iran's sole Nobel Prize winner · 伊朗唯一的诺贝尔奖得主
共和国
A young judge in the last years of the old regime; purged from the judiciary together with all women after the revolution. Opened her own law firm specializing in human rights, especially cases involving women or children. Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2003.
旧政权末期的年轻女法官，革命后与所有女性一起被清除出司法系统。后自行开设律师事务所，专注于人权案件，尤其是涉及妇女和儿童的案件。2003年荣获诺贝尔和平奖。
Farmanfarma, Firuz (Nowsrat al-Dowleh) 菲鲁兹·法尔曼法尔马（"国家的胜利"） 1889–1937
Prominent notable; helped Reza Shah rise to power · 权贵名流；助礼萨·沙阿登基
卡扎尔 巴列维
A scion of the famous Farmanfarma family and descendant of Fath Ali Shah. One of three who helped Reza Shah establish a strong centralized state — who eventually imprisoned and murdered him. In prison, he translated Oscar Wilde's De Profundis.
法尔曼法尔马名门之后，法特赫·阿里·沙阿的后裔。曾是协助礼萨·沙阿建立中央集权国家的三人之一，最终却被后者投入监狱并杀害。在狱中翻译了奥斯卡·王尔德的《自深深处》。
Fatemi, Sayyed Hussein 赛义德·侯赛因·法特米 1919–1954
Mossadeq's right-hand man; executed by the shah · 摩萨台的左膀右臂；被国王处决
巴列维
French-educated journalist and vocal supporter of oil nationalization. Served Mossadeq as foreign minister. Arrested after the coup and executed for "insulting the royal family" and plotting to establish a republic. Regarded as a hero of the nationalist movement — one of the few National Front leaders with a street named after him by the Islamic Republic.
留法记者，石油国有化运动的积极倡导者。曾任摩萨台的外交部长。政变后被捕，以"侮辱王室"和图谋建立共和国的罪名被处决。被视为民族主义运动的英雄——伊斯兰共和国为数不多以其命名街道的民族阵线领袖之一。
Hoveida, Abbas 阿巴斯·霍韦达 1919–1979
The shah's longest-lasting premier · 国王在位最久的首相
巴列维
Career public servant raised in a Bahai family (though not a practicing Bahai himself). Appointed premier in 1965 when his patron was assassinated by religious fanatics; remained until 1977. The shah first dismissed, then arrested him to mollify the opposition. One of the first executed by the revolutionary regime.
职业公务员，出身巴哈伊家庭（本人并非信徒）。1965年其庇护者遭宗教极端分子暗杀后被任命为首相，任职至1977年。国王为安抚反对派先将其免职后又下令逮捕。系革命政权最早处决的人之一。
Iskandari, Mirza Sulayman 米尔扎·苏莱曼·伊斯坎达里 1862–1944
Qajar prince prominent in the socialist movement · 活跃于社会主义运动的卡扎尔王子
卡扎尔 知识分子
Opponent of royal despotism for half a century. Participated in the Constitutional Revolution; led the Democrat Party (1909–21); imprisoned by the British in WWI; headed the Socialist Party (1921–26); returned to politics in 1941 to chair the Tudeh Party.
半个世纪的反专制斗士。参与立宪革命；领导民主党（1909–21）；一战中被英国人关押；主持社会党（1921–26）；1941年重返政坛出任人民党主席。
Kashani, Ayatollah Sayyed Abul-Qassem 阿亚图拉·赛义德·阿布·卡塞姆·卡沙尼 1885–1961
Main cleric who first supported, then opposed Mossadeq · 先支持后反对摩萨台的主要教士
教士 巴列维
Refugee from Iraq; arrested by the British in WWII. Threw his weight behind Mossadeq during oil nationalization; broke with him in 1953, avowedly because Mossadeq did not implement the shari'a. His supporters vehemently deny he actively supported the 1953 coup.
伊拉克流亡者，其父在一战后抗英斗争中牺牲；二战中被英国逮捕。在石油国有化运动期间全力支持摩萨台，1953年与之决裂，公开理由为后者未推行沙里亚法。其支持者激烈否认他积极参与了1953年政变。
Kasravi, Sayyed Ahmad 赛义德·艾哈迈德·卡斯拉维 1890–1946
Leading historian of modern Iran · 现代伊朗首要历史学家
知识分子 巴列维
Staunch advocate of national solidarity; persistently denounced all forms of communalism and sectarianism, including Shi'ism. Most controversial work: Shi'igari (Shi'i-mongering). Denounced as an "unbeliever" and assassinated. Yet Khomeini kept Kasravi's History of the Iranian Constitution on his shelves.
国家团结的坚定倡导者，始终抨击包括什叶派在内的一切宗派主义和社群主义。最具争议的著作是《什叶派主义》（Shi'igari）。被斥为"异教徒"后遭暗杀。然而霍梅尼始终将卡斯拉维的《伊朗立宪史》留在书架上。
Khamenei, Ayatollah Sayyed Ali 阿亚图拉·赛义德·阿里·哈梅内伊 1939–
Khomeini's successor as Supreme Leader · 霍梅尼的继任最高领袖
教士 共和国
From a minor clerical family in Azerbaijan; studied theology in Mashhad and Qom with Khomeini. Rose to prominence only after the revolution, holding a series of high positions including the presidency. Immediately after Khomeini's death, the regime elevated him to ayatollah and hailed him as the new Supreme Leader. He inherited Khomeini's powers but not his charisma.
出身阿塞拜疆省小教士家庭，先后在马什哈德和库姆随霍梅尼修习神学。革命后方崭露头角，历任多个要职包括总统。霍梅尼去世后，政权立即将其擢升为阿亚图拉并拥戴为新任最高领袖。他继承了霍梅尼的权力，却未能继承其个人魅力。
Khatemi, Hojjat al-Islam Sayyed Muhammad 霍贾特伊斯兰·赛义德·穆罕默德·哈塔米 1944–
Liberal reform president (1997 & 2001) · 自由派改革总统（1997、2001年两度当选）
教士 共和国
Son of an ayatollah close to Khomeini; studied theology in Qom and philosophy at Isfahan University. At the revolution's start, he was administering the Shi'i mosque in Hamburg. After the revolution, he headed publishing, sat in the Majles, and as culture minister aroused conservative anger by relaxing censorship. Won the presidency twice with landslide victories on a reform platform.
其父为霍梅尼好友阿亚图拉，曾在库姆修习神学并在伊斯法罕大学研读哲学，兼通英德两语。革命初期在汉堡主持什叶派清真寺。革命后先后负责出版工作、出任马杰利斯议员，任文化部长期间因放松书刊和电影审查而激怒保守派。以改革纲领两度赢得总统大选，均以压倒性优势胜出。
Khomeini, Ayatollah Sayyed Ruhollah 阿亚图拉·赛义德·鲁霍拉·霍梅尼 1902–1989
Charismatic leader of the Islamic Revolution · 伊斯兰革命的魅力型领袖
教士 革命 共和国
Born into a clerical family; spent early life in seminaries in Qom and Najaf. Entered politics in 1963 denouncing the shah for granting "capitulations" to American military advisors. Deported, he spent sixteen years in Najaf developing a new interpretation of Shi'i Islam — drastically expanding velayat-e faqeh from clerical jurisdiction over orphans, widows, and the mentally feeble to supervision over all citizens. Combined clerical conservatism with radical populism. Returning triumphant in 1979, he was hailed as Commander of the Revolution, Founder of the Islamic Republic, Supreme Leader, and — most potent — Imam of the Muslim World, a title Shi'is had reserved for the Twelve Sacred Infallible Imams.
生于教士家庭，早年在库姆和纳杰夫的神学院中度过。1963年因公开谴责国王授予美国军事顾问"领事裁判权"而步入政坛。遭驱逐后在纳杰夫流亡十六年，期间发展出对什叶派伊斯兰的全新诠释——将"法基赫监护权"（velayat-e faqeh）从教士对孤儿、寡妇和心智缺陷者的有限管辖权，大幅扩展为对全体公民的监护权。将教士阶层的保守主义与激进的民粹主义熔于一炉。1979年凯旋归国，被新宪法尊为革命统帅、伊斯兰共和国缔造者、最高领袖，以及最具威力的头衔——穆斯林世界的伊玛目——而什叶派过去仅将此称号赋予十二位神圣无谬的伊玛目。
Modarres, Sayyed Hassan 赛义德·哈桑·穆达雷斯 1870–1937
Main ayatollah opposed to Reza Shah · 反对礼萨·沙阿的主要阿亚图拉
教士 巴列维
Majles member since 1914; known chiefly as a parliamentary politician. Opposed the 1919 Anglo-Persian Agreement and tried to stem the rise of Reza Shah. Banished to the provinces and eventually murdered there. Modern Islamists view him as one of their forerunners.
1914年起任马杰利斯议员，以议会政治家著称。反对1919年英波协定并试图遏制礼萨·沙阿的崛起。被流放外省后遭杀害。现代伊斯兰主义者视其为先驱之一。
Mossadeq, Muhammad (Mossadeq al-Saltaneh) 穆罕默德·摩萨台 1881–1967
The icon of Iranian nationalism · 伊朗民族主义的标志性人物
巴列维
From a long line of notables; studied in Europe; forced into retirement by Reza Shah. Returned in 1941, gaining fame as an "incorruptible" deputy, then as leader of the National Front campaigning for oil nationalization. Elected prime minister in 1951, he nationalized the oil industry and sparked a major international crisis with Britain. Overthrown by the CIA coup in August 1953. Islamists distrusted him for his deep commitment to secular nationalism.
出身世代权贵家族，留学欧洲，被礼萨·沙阿强迫退休。1941年复出，先以"不可腐蚀"的议员闻名，继而作为民族阵线领袖领导石油国有化运动。1951年当选首相后迅即推行石油国有化，引发与英国的重大国际危机。1953年8月被中情局策划的政变推翻。伊斯兰主义者因其对世俗民族主义的坚定信仰而不信任他。
Muhammad Reza Shah Pahlavi 穆罕默德·礼萨·沙阿·巴列维 1919–1980
Monarch overthrown by the Islamic Revolution · 被伊斯兰革命推翻的君主
巴列维
Raised to be commander-in-chief of the armed forces. Ascended the throne in 1941; consolidated power after the 1953 CIA coup, ruling much like his father and using oil revenues to expand the state and military. Died soon after the revolution — from cancer kept secret even from his own family so as not to endanger the regime. Described as a "majestic failure."
自幼被培养为武装部队最高统帅。1941年登基；1953年中情局政变后巩固权力，执政风格酷似其父，利用石油收入大规模扩张国家机器和军事力量。革命后不久即因癌症去世——为不危及政权，他甚至对家人也隐瞒了病情。被形容为一个"壮丽的失败者"。
Navab-Safavi, Sayyed Mojtaba Mir-Lowhi 赛义德·穆杰塔巴·纳瓦布–萨法维 1922–1956
Founder of Fedayan-e Islam · "伊斯兰敢死队"（Fedayan-e Islam）创始人
教士 巴列维
Founded one of the first truly fundamentalist organizations in Iran. The group carried out high-profile assassinations between 1944 and 1952; also attempted to kill Mossadeq's advisor and plotted against Mossadeq himself. Executed in 1956 after an assassination attempt on the then PM. The far rightists among Khomeini's followers deem Safavi a forerunner.
创建了伊朗最早的原教旨主义组织之一。该组织在1944至1952年间实施了多起轰动暗杀事件，还企图杀害摩萨台的主要顾问并密谋刺杀摩萨台本人。1956年在一次未遂暗杀后被处决。霍梅尼追随者中的极右翼视其为先驱。
Nuri, Sheikh Fazlollah 谢赫·法兹洛拉·努里 1843–1909
Leading cleric opposed to the Constitutional Revolution · 反对立宪革命的主要教士
教士 卡扎尔
Initially supported attempts to limit royal power, but grew fearful of secularists and sided with the royalists. Issued fatwas accusing reformers of being Babis, atheists, and freethinkers — some were killed. Hanged after the civil war for issuing such lethal fatwas. The modern Islamist movement regards him as one of their very first "martyrs."
起初支持限制王权的努力，后因惧怕世俗派而倒向保王派。发布法特瓦指控改革者为巴布教徒、无神论者和自由思想者——部分人因此丧命。内战后因颁布致命法特瓦被处以绞刑。现代伊斯兰主义运动视其为最早的"殉道者"之一。
Qavam, Ahmad (Qavam al-Saltaneh) 艾哈迈德·卡瓦姆 1877–1955
Most notable of the old notables · 旧式权贵中最显赫者
卡扎尔 巴列维
Court-supporter of the 1906 revolution — the proclamation granting the constitution reportedly was written in his calligraphic hand. A highly successful wheeler and dealer: four cabinets before Reza Shah and six after. Some credit him — rather than Truman — with persuading Stalin to withdraw the Red Army from Azerbaijan. His reputation was tarnished after offering to replace Mossadeq in 1952. Vossuq's younger brother.
1906年革命的宫廷支持者——据传授予宪法的御诏即出自其手书。极为成功的纵横家：在礼萨·沙阿前后共组阁十次。有人认为是他（而非杜鲁门）说服斯大林从阿塞拜疆撤走红军。1952年主动提出取代摩萨台出任首相，名声因此受损。为沃苏格之弟。
Rafsanjani, Hojjat al-Islam Ali-Akbar Hashemi 霍贾特伊斯兰·阿里–阿克巴尔·哈什米·拉夫桑贾尼 1934–2017
The éminence grise of the Islamic Republic · 伊斯兰共和国的"灰衣主教"
教士 共和国
Born into a prosperous agricultural family; studied with Khomeini in Qom; in and out of prison during the 1960s. Wrote a book praising a 19th-century minister who tried to industrialize Iran. After the revolution, held numerous positions including presidency and chairmanship of the Expediency Council. Considered the most important person after the Supreme Leader.
出身富裕农业家庭，在库姆师从霍梅尼，1960年代数度入狱。曾撰书赞扬一位试图推动伊朗工业化的19世纪大臣。革命后出任多个要职，包括总统和确定国家利益委员会主席。被认为是仅次于最高领袖的最重要人物。
Reza Shah Pahlavi 礼萨·沙阿·巴列维 1878–1944
Founder of Iran's centralized state · 伊朗中央集权国家的缔造者
军事 巴列维
Born into a military family; rose through the Cossack Brigade. Led the 1921 coup; crowned himself shah in 1926, replacing the Qajars with the Pahlavi dynasty. Ruled with an iron fist until 1941, when the Anglo-Soviet invasion forced his abdication. Died in South Africa. Left his son the crown and a huge private fortune — considered one of the largest in the Middle East.
军人家庭出身，在哥萨克旅中逐步晋升。1921年发动政变，1926年自行加冕为王，以巴列维王朝取代卡扎尔王朝。以铁腕统治至1941年英苏联合入侵迫其逊位。卒于南非。给儿子留下王冠和一笔巨额私人财富——当时被认为是中东最大的私人资产之一。
Rouhani, Hassan 哈桑·鲁哈尼 1948–
Moderate cleric elected president (2013 & 2017) · 温和派教士总统（2013、2017年当选）
教士 共和国
Born into a non-clerical family in a village east of Tehran; educated mostly in Qom and briefly in Glasgow. One of the youngest clerics to rally behind Khomeini; rose to the uppermost levels of the regime before being elected president.
出生于德黑兰以东一个非教士家庭的村落，主要在库姆受教育，曾短暂就读于格拉斯哥。系最早响应霍梅尼号召的年轻教士之一，在新政权中晋升至最高层，后当选总统。
Sardar As'ad Bakhtiyari, Jafar Quli Khan 萨达尔·阿萨德·巴赫蒂亚里 1882–1934
Crucial figure in the civil war · 内战中的关键人物
卡扎尔 军事
He and fellow Bakhtiyari chiefs led tribal contingents into Tehran, giving the constitutionalists a decisive victory. They turned some ministries into family fiefdoms until ousted by Reza Shah. Murdered in prison.
率巴赫蒂亚里部落武装攻入德黑兰，为立宪派赢得决定性胜利。此后将若干部会变为家族领地，直至被礼萨·沙阿清除。死于狱中。
Sayyed Ziya (al-Din Tabatabai) 赛义德·齐亚 1889–1969
Pro-British politician behind the 1921 coup · 1921年政变中的亲英政客
卡扎尔 巴列维
An openly pro-British journalist; appointed PM by Reza Khan in 1921, only to be ousted months later. After twenty years in exile, returned in WWII and made numerous attempts to become PM again — often with British support but always with Soviet and American opposition. Had regular private audiences with the shah until his death.
公开亲英的记者；1921年被礼萨汗任命为首相，几个月后即被免职。流亡二十年后于二战期间回国，此后多次试图重任首相——常获英国支持，但总遭苏联和美国反对。直至去世一直定期与国王私下会面。
Sepahdar, Muhammad Vali Khan 穆罕默德·瓦利汗·塞帕赫达尔 1847–1926
Crucial figure in the civil war · 内战中的关键人物
卡扎尔
A major landlord in Mazanderan and nominal commander of the royal army; defected to the constitutionalists, helping seal the monarchists' fate. Headed eight cabinets between 1910 and 1919. Committed suicide in anticipation of incarceration by Reza Shah.
马赞达兰的大地主，名义上的王室军队统帅；倒戈加入立宪派，为保王派的覆灭起了决定性作用。1910至1919年间先后组阁八次。预感将被礼萨·沙阿监禁而自杀身亡。
Shariati, Ali 阿里·沙里亚蒂 1933–1977
"The real ideologue" of the Islamic Revolution · 伊斯兰革命的"真正思想家"
知识分子 革命
Studying in France in the 1960s, he was strongly influenced by Third World revolution theorists, especially Frantz Fanon. His prolific lectures (some 35 volumes) aimed to transform Shi'ism from a conservative apolitical religion into a revolutionary political ideology competing with Leninism and Maoism. His writings influenced many revolution activists. Died in exile on the eve of the revolution.
1960年代留学法国，深受第三世界革命理论家尤其是弗朗茨·法农的影响。其大量演讲（约三十五卷）旨在将什叶派从保守的非政治宗教改造为可与列宁主义和毛泽东主义相抗衡的革命性政治意识形态。著作影响了众多革命活动者。革命前夕客死异乡。
Tabatabai, Sayyed Muhammad Sadeq 赛义德·穆罕默德·萨德格·塔巴塔巴伊 1841–1918
Ayatollah prominent in the Constitutional Revolution · 立宪革命中的重要阿亚图拉
教士 卡扎尔
A secret freemason who played a leading role throughout the revolution; fled after the 1908 coup. His son became a major powerbroker in the Majles in the 1940s.
秘密共济会成员，在整个立宪革命中发挥核心作用；1908年政变后流亡。其子在1940年代成为马杰利斯中举足轻重的政治掮客。
Taleqani, Ayatollah Sayyed Mahmud 阿亚图拉·赛义德·马哈茂德·塔莱加尼 1919–1979
Most popular cleric in Tehran during the revolution · 革命期间德黑兰最受爱戴的教士
教士 革命
Consistent supporter of Mossadeq and founding member of the Liberation Movement. Enjoyed good rapport with all opposition segments — National Front, Mojahedin, and even Marxists. Organized the mass rallies of 1978. Had he not died soon after the revolution, he might have provided a liberal counterweight to Khomeini.
摩萨台的坚定支持者，解放运动创始成员。与反对派各方——民族阵线、穆贾赫丁乃至马克思主义团体——均保持良好关系。组织了1978年的大规模集会。若非革命后不久即去世，他或许能成为制衡霍梅尼的自由派力量。
Taqizadeh, Sayyed Hassan 赛义德·哈桑·塔基扎德 1874–1970
Leading intellectual–politician of the Constitutional Revolution · 立宪革命核心知识分子–政治家
知识分子 卡扎尔 巴列维
Firebrand deputy in the First Majles speaking for the Democrat Party; aroused the wrath of conservative ulama, who issued fatwas against him. Lived in exile 1909–1924; returned to hold positions under Reza Shah and Muhammad Reza Shah. Some see him as typifying young radicals coopted into the Pahlavi regime.
第一届马杰利斯中代表民主党发言的激进议员，激怒保守派乌莱玛，遭其发布法特瓦。1909至1924年流亡海外，回国后先后在礼萨·沙阿和穆罕默德·礼萨·沙阿手下任职。有人视其为被巴列维政权收编的激进青年一代的典型。
Vossuq, Mirza Hassan Khan (Vossuq al-Dowleh) 米尔扎·哈桑汗·沃苏格 1865–1951
Signatory to the notorious 1919 Anglo-Persian Agreement · 臭名昭著的1919年英波协定的签署者
卡扎尔
A prominent notable who served in numerous administrations between 1909 and 1926. He was Qavam's older brother.
显赫权贵，1909至1926年间在多届政府中任职。卡瓦姆之兄。
Yeprem Khan (Davitian) 叶普雷姆汗（达维提安） 1868–1912
Leader of Caucasian fighters in the civil war · 内战中高加索志愿军领袖
卡扎尔 军事
Member of the Armenian Dashnak Party; sent to Siberia, escaped to Iran. Led Caucasian volunteers fighting for the constitutionalists under the slogan "The Love of Freedom has No Fatherland." Appointed police chief of Tehran after the war; killed fighting insurgents.
亚美尼亚达什纳克党成员；曾被流放西伯利亚，后逃至伊朗。率高加索志愿者在"自由之爱无祖国"的口号下为立宪派作战。内战后被任命为德黑兰警察局长，在镇压叛乱中阵亡。
Zahedi, General Fazlollah 法兹洛拉·扎赫迪将军 1897–1963
Nominal head of the 1953 CIA coup · 1953年中情局政变的名义领导人
军事 巴列维
Career officer from the Cossack Brigade; in and out of favor with both Reza Shah and Muhammad Reza Shah. Imprisoned by the British in 1942 for contacts with the Third Reich. Appointed PM in 1953; eased out by the shah in 1955. Moved to exile and died in his luxury mansion in Switzerland.
哥萨克旅出身的职业军官，与两代巴列维国王时合时离。1942年因与第三帝国有接触被英国关押。1953年被任命为首相，1955年被国王架空。流亡海外，卒于其在瑞士的豪宅。
